MY STORY

In 2017 I had graduated high school and was attending my local community college to pursue a degree in computer science. As I began to dive deeper into code, I started to feel I could really accelerate my learning. I decided to leave my college and enroll in a local coding bootcamp called Codeup. Fortunately, I was able to receive an offer before the bootcamp was over from Accenture.

After a short while at Accenture, I found a unique startup that really intersted me. It was a small company specializing in Fintech, and they had just been aquired by a tech giant called Fiserv. My time there was pretty amazing. I learned so many new techniques and really got to collaborate with a cool team.

That brings me to now. I am in my last week Fiserv. I have accepted a new opportunity to work with a small team and I am extremely exited. I guess we will see what happens from here!
